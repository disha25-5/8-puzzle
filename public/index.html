Replica of the Chopping hack that is triggered when chopping cars on NoPixel 4.0

Roof Running

Replica of the Roof Running hack that is triggered when robbing AC-Units on NoPixel 4.0

Laundromat

Replica of the Laundromat hack that is triggered when robbing the safe inside the south-side Laundromat on NoPixel 4.0

LockPick

Replica of the LockPick hack that is triggered when lockpicking vehicles, among other things, on NoPixel 4.0

RepairKit

Replica of the RepairKit hack that is triggered when repairing vehicles on NoPixel 4.0

SmokeCrack

Replica of the SmokeCrack hack that is triggered through the in-game PC when hacking Wi-Fi's on NoPixel 4.0
